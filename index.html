The Challenge

Olive oil markets are complex and volatile, influenced by weather patterns, geopolitical events, supply chain disruptions, and seasonal variations. Traditional forecasting methods lack the sophistication to capture these multi-dimensional factors, leaving buyers, traders, and agricultural businesses without reliable market intelligence.

Price Volatility

Olive oil prices can fluctuate 20-40% within months due to unpredictable factors

Market Complexity

Multiple countries, grades, and supply chain factors create complex market dynamics

Limited Intelligence

Existing tools lack AI-powered insights and conversational accessibility

Our Solution

A comprehensive multi-agent AI platform that combines advanced machine learning forecasting with conversational intelligence, providing both technical depth for analysts and natural language accessibility for business users.

ML Forecasting Engine

Advanced machine learning models deployed on AWS SageMaker provide weekly price predictions with confidence intervals, incorporating historical data, seasonality, and market drivers.

Conversational AI Assistant

AWS Bedrock-powered agent provides natural language access to market intelligence, answering questions about prices, trends, and market drivers in plain English.

Unified Dashboard

Streamlit-based interface combining technical ML controls with conversational AI for comprehensive market analysis.

Value Proposition

Empower retail and manufacturing procurement teams to make faster decisions and anticipate market shocks with AI-powered price forecasts, market intelligence, and supplier comparisons that optimize purchasing decisions, reduce costs, and mitigate supply chain risk.

The Data

Multi-source dataset combining retail prices, macroeconomic indicators, freight indices, and tariff schedules, creating a comprehensive week-by-week view of global olive oil markets.

Data Sources
  • EU Market Observatory: Weekly retail/wholesale olive oil prices by grade and country
  • FRED (Federal Reserve): Daily EUR/USD exchange rates for price alignment
  • Freightos Baltic Index (FBX): Weekly ocean freight benchmarks
  • EIA: Weekly U.S. diesel prices for transportation costs
  • USITC: U.S. duty rates and tariff schedules
  • BLS PPI: Monthly packaging material costs (glass, plastic, steel)
Automated Data Pipeline

Fully automated, quality-controlled processing:

  • Format Standardization: Unified data schemas across sources
  • Temporal Alignment: Weekly timestamp synchronization
  • Range Validation: Automated quality checks and anomaly detection
  • ML-Ready Output: Clean economic panel for forecasting and supply-chain insights

The Model

Advanced machine learning architecture deployed on AWS SageMaker for scalable, production-ready forecasting.

Ridge Regression

Regularized linear model that handles multicollinearity in market drivers while maintaining interpretability for business stakeholders.

  • Handles correlated features effectively
  • Provides confidence intervals for predictions
  • Interpretable coefficients for market analysis
Feature Engineering

Sophisticated preprocessing pipeline that captures temporal patterns and market dynamics.

  • Lag features for time series patterns
  • Rolling averages for trend smoothing
  • Seasonal indicators for cyclical patterns
  • Market driver interactions
AWS SageMaker Deployment

Production-ready ML pipeline with automated training, versioning, and real-time inference endpoints for scalable predictions.
